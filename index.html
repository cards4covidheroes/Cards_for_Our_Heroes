A special thanks to our frontline healthcare workers!

Hello! Our names are Mantej Singh and Prabhleen Kaur and we are students at Washington High School. Especially during the COVID-19 crisis, healthcare workers are working long hours to help save the lives of millions of people worldwide. They are our true heroes because they put their lives at risk everyday to protect ours. In order to show our appreciation, we are collecting thank you cards and putting them together with gift cards to deliver to hospitals.
