A special thanks to our frontline healthcare workers!

Hello! Our names are Mantej Singh Lamba and Prabhleen Kaur Lamba and we are students at Washington High School. Especially during the COVID-19 crisis, healthcare workers are working long hours to help save the lives of millions of people worldwide. They are our true heroes because they put their lives at risk everyday to protect ours. To show our appreciation, we are collecting thank you cards and sending them to hospitals along with gift cards. Special thanks to Blackhawk Network for their sponsorship and helping us purchase gift cards! Here is a picture of the customized gift card we will be sending along with the thank you cards:
