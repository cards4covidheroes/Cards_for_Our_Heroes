Thank You!

favorite local_florist local_hospital favorite local_florist local_hospital

We want to thank everyone who either made cards or donated to our GoFundMe page. The amount of support we have received from the community has been absolutely incredible. None of this would have been possible without your amazing support. We would also like to give our thanks to the Fremont Unified School District staff. By spreading our message to parents and students, we were able to collect even more cards and donations. Our project is not done yet, and we are still looking forward to receiving more cards. Please continue to spread the word so we can honor those who are serving us in these uncertain times. Stay safe everyone and once again, thank you!
